Sara Namdarian

About

Sara Namdarian studied Interior Architecture two years in Malaysia and London, the metropolitan city of art and creativity.She came back to Malaysia in 2009 and pursue teaching architecture at Limkokwing University of Creative Technology. She also obtained her M.A. in Architecture science in Virtual/Augmented Reality in architecture in University Malaya.

She entered the Interior Architecture industry in 2013 by establishing her own design firm in Melaka. After working 4 years in Melaka, she moved to Kuala Lumpur to explore a bigger market for her fashion and interior works.
