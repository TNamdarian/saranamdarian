About

sara studied Interior Architecture in Malaysia and London.

She pursued teaching architecture at Limkokwing University of Creative Technology in 2009. She also obtained her M.A. in Architecture science in Virtual/Augmented Reality in architecture in University Malaya.

She entered the Interior Architecture industry in 2013 by establishing her own design firm in Melaka. After working 4 years in Melaka, she moved to Kuala Lumpur to explore a bigger market for her fashion and interior works.
